# Chanson à Répondre UNO 2.0

## Version 2.0.0

- Adds the five official deck categories: Classique, Sauvage, Poésie, Cyberpunk, and Art contemporain.
- Adds category badges on card displays and category filters in Browse/Search.
- Adds supplied category verso artwork for face-down cards in the Play path.
- Keeps the permanent 67-card bundled deck public, available at startup, and free of import/export activation.
- Keeps Browse limited to five visible cards at a time with Previous/Next navigation, shuffle, search, and filters preserved.
- Keeps the Play hand in a five-card visible lane with scrolling for additional drawn cards.
- Adds Search-result thumbnails; long-press/long-click opens the card fullscreen.
